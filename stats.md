# 🇮🇳 **Stats to remember for SIH - Cheat sheet**

---

## **1) India’s Edible Oil Snapshot**

* **Per-capita consumption:** ~**19.3 kg/person/year** (commonly used benchmark).
* Alternate survey estimate: **~13.6 kg/person/year**.
* **Recommended (ICMR):** **12 kg/year**.
* **Total national use (2023–24):** ~**27.8 million tonnes**.
* **Imports:** ~**15.96–16 million tonnes/year**.
* **Import dependency:** ~**56%** of India’s needs.
* **Import bill:** **₹1.32 lakh crore/year** (~₹1.32 trillion).

## **4) Economic Consequences**

* High imports reduce **foreign exchange reserves**.
* Cheaper imported oils → **lower profitability** for Indian farmers.
* Domestic oilseed cultivation becomes less attractive → lower rural incomes.
* Reducing consumption by **10%** = major savings + strengthens Atmanirbhar Bharat.

---

## **5) Health Risks of Excess Oil Intake**

* **Obesity / weight gain**
* **Type-2 diabetes**
* **Heart disease & stroke**
* **High cholesterol (↑ LDL, ↓ HDL)**
* **Hypertension**
* **Fatty liver (NAFLD)**
* Inflammation & metabolic syndrome

---

## **6) Dietary Preventive Measures**

* Reduce overall oil intake (avoid deep frying).
* Use **low-oil cooking**: steam, bake, grill, sauté.
* Prefer **MUFA/PUFA-rich oils** (olive, mustard, groundnut, rice-bran).
* Avoid **reusing frying oil**.
* Use **spoon-based measured pouring**.
* Follow NIN/ICMR balanced-diet guidelines.
* Monitor: **BMI, sugar, lipid profile**.
